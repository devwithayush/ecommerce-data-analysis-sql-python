# E-Commerce Data Analysis Report

## Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze a real-world e-commerce dataset and understand key aspects of the business such as revenue patterns, customer behavior, delivery performance, and product demand.

---

## Dataset Source

I used the **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist** from Kaggle.
It contains real-world data with **100K+ orders**, **90K+ customers**, and **110K+ order items** from a Brazilian e-commerce platform.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:

* Orders data (purchase and delivery timestamps)
* Customer information (location and IDs)
* Product-level transaction data
* Payment details

After cleaning and merging, I created a final dataset with **100K+ transaction-level records** for analysis.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Behavior

* Total revenue generated is around **16M+**
* Most transactions fall below **500**
* Only a small number of high-value transactions exist
* This shows the business depends on **high-volume, low-value orders**

---

### 2. Customer Behavior

* Around **90%+ customers** placed only one order
* Repeat purchase rate is **less than 10%**
* This clearly indicates **very low customer retention**

---

### 3. Order Trends

* Orders increased consistently over time
* Peak activity was observed during **late 2017 to early 2018**
* This indicates strong growth and increasing adoption of the platform

---

### 4. Delivery Performance

* Average delivery time is around **10–15 days**
* Most deliveries are completed within **5–20 days**
* Around **7–8% of orders are delivered late**
* Even this small delay percentage can impact customer satisfaction

---

### 5. Product Performance

* A small number of products contribute to a large portion of total sales
* Top products dominate demand significantly
* This shows a **skewed distribution (Pareto-like behavior)**

---

### 6. Geographic Distribution

* São Paulo (SP) contributes the highest number of customers
* Other states like RJ and MG follow but at a lower scale
* Customer base is highly concentrated in a few regions

---

## Key Metrics

* Total Orders: **100K+**
* Total Customers: **90K+**
* Total Revenue: **~16M+**
* Average Order Value (AOV): **~150–160**
* Late Delivery Rate: **~7–8%**
* Repeat Customer Rate: **<10%**

---

## Business Recommendations

* Improve customer retention through loyalty programs and offers
* Reduce delivery delays to improve customer experience
* Focus on top-performing products and expand similar categories
* Target high-demand regions for marketing campaigns
* Encourage repeat purchases through personalized engagement

---

## Conclusion

In this project, I worked on a complete data analysis pipeline including data cleaning, merging, and analysis.

The analysis helped identify key issues such as **low customer retention (<10%)** and **delivery delays (~7–8%)**, while also highlighting opportunities like focusing on high-performing products and regions.

Overall, this project demonstrates how real-world data can be used to generate meaningful business insights and support decision-making.
